Stair

33.1 Effective Span of Stairs

The effective span of stairs without stringer beams shall be taken as the following horizontal distances:

  1. Where supported at top and bottom risers by beams spanning parallel with the risers, the distance center-to-center of beams;
  2. Where spanning on to the edge of a landing slab, which spans parallel, with the risers (see Fig. 17), a distance equal to the going of the stairs plus at each end either half the width of the landing or one meter, whichever is smaller; and

33.3 Depth of Section

The depth of section shall be taken as the minimum thickness perpendicular to the soffit of the staircase.
